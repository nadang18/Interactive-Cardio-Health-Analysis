Alcohol Consumption & Cardiac Risk: Insights from the MUSIC Study

The MUSIC (MUerte Subita en Insuficiencia Cardiaca) study investigates key risk factors for cardiac mortality and sudden cardiac death (SCD) in chronic heart failure (CHF) patients. The visualization below explores the relationship between alcohol consumption levels, survival outcomes, and patient demographics from heart clinics at the University Spanish Hospital.

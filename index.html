Cardiovascular Health: Risk Factors Assessment Analysis

The MUSIC (MUerte Subita en Insuficiencia Cardiaca) study was a research project aimed at identifying risk predictors for cardiac mortality and sudden cardiac death (SCD) in chronic heart failure (CHF) patients. Displayed below are record of patients from heart clinics within the University Spanish Hospital.
